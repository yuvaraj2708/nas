    Express Service

    Some shipments need extra special care. With our on board courier service, our specialist air freight staff can fly to your required destination on the first available flight and personally deliver and hand over the goods, if necessary.

    Charter services

    We can organize special flights, chartering an entire plane in which your goods are the sole priority, to send large batches of goods swiftly and quickly to your required destination, should there be space constraints in high season or limited capacity issues. Alternatively, we can send a plane to any airport in the world to collect your goods and deliver them to your facilities or your customer’s premises.
